Frequently Asked Questions

How fast is the vacancy published?

After filling out the form, our team moderates it. Usually, the vacancy appears on the site within 24 hours.

What is Rybezh Proof and why is it needed?

Rybezh Proof is our trust rating system. We collect feedback from candidates about working conditions. A high rating significantly increases the number of applications for your vacancy.

Can I change the description after publication?

Yes, you can contact our support via Telegram at any time, and we will promptly make the necessary changes to your ad.
