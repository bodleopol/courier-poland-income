Professional overview

Prime Grid sells Product platform capabilities to teams scaling past product-market fit. Core offer: a B2B platform with APIs, access roles, and analytics for product teams; founded 2013, based in San Francisco.

GTM relies on pilots with explicit KPIs—onboarding time, automated workflow share, hours saved. The company competes on SLA predictability and implementation support more than on rock-bottom pricing.

This overview is not an investment memo; it records why the card sits in “software” and which questions to ask founders or IR on a first call.
