Cookies policy

The site uses only basic technical storage for interface theme and the cookies notice choice.

1. What is used

The site uses only a technical storage mechanism for theme preference and cookies-notice choice. It is not behavioural advertising or third-party tracking.

2. Why it is used

Saving theme preference keeps the interface stable between visits, while saving the notice choice prevents the banner from reappearing unnecessarily.

3. How to opt out

You can clear localStorage in the browser or decline through the banner. The site will still work, but theme preference and cookies choice will not persist.
