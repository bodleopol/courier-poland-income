About us

Rybezh.site is a multilingual editorial directory of people and technology companies. We prioritise checkable facts, a consistent card layout, and a calm tone so you can compare context without noise.

Our mission

Give readers a steady map of roles, geography, and mandate: who is responsible for what, where the team works, which public themes they emphasise, and where to go for primary sources.

We do not compete with news feeds and we do not replace official sites—if a source disagrees with us, trust the source.

Editorial backend

Harmonising field structure, tags, and translations across UA / EN / ES / RU so the same card reads predictably in every language.

Fact-check & sources

Review of public bios, roles, and product statements; page updates when the public record changes.

Card quality

Checks on imagery, mandate summaries, and links—minimal decorative noise, maximum repeatable structure for comparison.

Editorial principles

Transparency. Every page exposes role, region, focus, tags, and paths to primary material.

Respect for readers. No clickbait phrasing and no filler pretending to be substance.

Separate streams. People profiles and company profiles live in different catalogues so mandates do not blur.

How we verify

We rely on public sources (company sites, interviews, official bios, registries), align tone across languages, and refresh pages when context shifts.

Why people use this

Consistent card structure, predictable tags, and full-site search make it faster to find a person or company page without juggling mismatched templates.
