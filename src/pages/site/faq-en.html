Frequently asked questions

Common questions about the format, sourcing, and how to suggest a correction.

What is Rybezh.site?

A multilingual editorial directory of people and technology companies—short reference pages with an emphasis on context and pointers to primary sources.

Is this a news feed or a jobs board?

No. Pages are meant for orientation, not as a live news stream or recruiting channel.

Where do the facts come from?

We rely on public sources (company sites, interviews, official bios, filings). If the world moves on, check the primary source—it always wins when there is a conflict.

How do I suggest a fix or a topic?

Email jobs.r@protonmail.com with the page URL, what looks inaccurate, and a link to evidence when you can.

How often do pages change?

When public context shifts (roles, products, org charts). Larger rewrites follow the same editorial frame described under Methodology.
