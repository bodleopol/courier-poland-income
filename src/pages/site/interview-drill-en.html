Interview drill

Ten thousand unique combinations of context, stressor, role, competency and question archetype — for self-check before leadership and technical interviews. Each screen shows four prompts. There is also a short five-option drill with feedback after you pick an answer; open-ended prompts remain unscored.

How it works

This is a practice simulator, not a substitute for a real interview. Open-ended prompts are assembled combinatorially from curated lists and templates (see Methodology, section 5), so wording may feel similar across items, but each batch index is unique. A separate MCQ mode offers five choices and highlights the recommended learning answer after you select one. Notes are stored only in your browser (localStorage). Short answers to common questions are in the FAQ.

    Tip: ← / → switch batches when focus is not in a field.
