Ada Lovelace is known as Programming pioneer. Professional focus: algorithms, analytical engines, mathematical modelling. Key contribution: the first published algorithm for Charles Babbage's analytical engine. Her notes joined mathematics with a broader vision of machines that could work with symbols, music and ideas, not only numbers.
