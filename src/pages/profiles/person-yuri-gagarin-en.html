Professional overview

Yuri Gagarin is known as Cosmonaut. Professional focus: human spaceflight, pilot training, flight testing. Key contribution: the first human journey into outer space. The Vostok 1 flight made him a symbol of the space age and revealed the scale of engineering teams behind every mission.
