Life and contribution

Sergey Korolev is known as Chief designer of rocket and space systems. Professional focus: rocketry, systems engineering, space programmes. Key contribution: organising the first satellite launch and early crewed flights. He represents the systems engineer who combines science, manufacturing, risk and team coordination.
