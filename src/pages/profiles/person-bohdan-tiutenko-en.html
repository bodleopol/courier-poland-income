Professional overview

Bohdan Tiutenko is a highly skilled Chief Operating Officer (COO) with over 5 years of hands-on experience managing complex logistics and foodtech ecosystems. He specializes in scaling businesses, building operational processes from the ground up, and data-driven crisis management. His approach seamlessly combines a deep understanding of field operations with robust data analytics, enabling him to make effective managerial decisions and drive high business performance.

Throughout his career, Bohdan has successfully led operations in hyper-growth environments. At Rocket Ukraine, he spearheaded city operations, where he built and coordinated a fleet of over 150 couriers from scratch, ensuring delivery stability and speed during peak hours. Later, managing projects in Lima, Peru, he optimized workflows across a network of dark kitchens. His work involved synchronizing food production with dispatch systems and implementing strict FIFO/PEPS standards, which significantly reduced operational costs and waste.

Bohdan's leadership style is fundamentally driven by numbers and well-defined processes. He has extensive experience managing P&L, developing KPI/OKR frameworks, and drafting Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs). His technical toolkit includes Power BI, SQL, advanced Excel, and modern CRM systems. As a leader, Bohdan is known for his transparency, practical mindset, and willingness to dive into the trenches. He excels at identifying operational bottlenecks on the ground and building systems that run like clockwork.
