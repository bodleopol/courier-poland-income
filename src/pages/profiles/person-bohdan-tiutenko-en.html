Professional overview

Bohdan Tiutenko is an Operations Executive (COO) with over 5 years of experience managing complex logistics and foodtech ecosystems, currently seeking new professional challenges as an Operations Director. His career is a unique blend of extreme crisis management and deep, data-driven analytics. Before entering the corporate sector, Bohdan served in the Main Directorate of Intelligence (GUR) during the 2014 events in Donbas, an experience he candidly documented in his memoir. This background forged his exceptional stress resistance, decisive decision-making in critical situations, and profound sense of responsibility for his team.

Transitioning into business, he successfully scaled operations during hyper-growth phases. At Rocket Ukraine, he led city operations, building and coordinating a fleet of over 150 couriers to ensure delivery stability during peak hours. Later, in Lima, Peru, he optimized dark kitchen workflows—from synchronizing production with dispatch systems to implementing strict FIFO/PEPS standards, significantly reducing operational losses. His approach is fundamentally driven by numbers and processes (P&L, KPI/OKR, SOPs), utilizing tools like Power BI, SQL, Excel, and CRM systems. As a leader, Bohdan values humanity, transparency, and a hands-on approach—he is always ready to dive into the trenches, identify bottlenecks, and build a system that runs like clockwork. He is fluent in Ukrainian, Russian, Spanish (C1), and English (B2).
