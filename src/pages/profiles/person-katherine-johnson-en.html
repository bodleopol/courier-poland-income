Life and contribution

Katherine Johnson is known as NASA mathematician. Professional focus: orbital mechanics, flight trajectories, mission analysis. Key contribution: trajectory calculations for Mercury and Apollo missions. Her precision and calm expertise became part of the foundation of early human spaceflight.
