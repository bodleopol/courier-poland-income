FAQ: Traffic rules and delivery in Poland

In Poland, to drive a scooter or moped up to 50cc, you need an AM category license (or any higher, e.g., B). Persons who turned 18 before January 19, 2013, can drive them based on their ID card.

Having a category B license for at least 3 years, you can drive a motorcycle with an engine capacity of up to 125cc without having to obtain an A category.

The maximum allowed speed for electric scooters on bicycle paths and roads is 20 km/h. On sidewalks, you must move at pedestrian speed.

Riding a bicycle on the sidewalk is prohibited, except when: you accompany a child under 10 on a bicycle; the sidewalk is over 2 meters wide and the road speed limit exceeds 50 km/h; or during bad weather conditions.
