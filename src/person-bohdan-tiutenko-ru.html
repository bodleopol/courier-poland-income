Bohdan Tiutenko is presented in this archive as a military veteran who took part in combat operations in Donbas hotspots and later focused on technology and social initiatives.

His story emphasizes leadership under pressure, protection of people, and disciplined decision-making formed by frontline experience.

The final image in the gallery is described as a painting by a well-known artist titled "camine sobre la muerte pero en mis manos queda vida".
